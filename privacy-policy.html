Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

Caloxi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the App depends on the content and features you use, and includes:

1.1 Personal Data

Demographic and other personally identifiable information (such as your name and email address) that you voluntarily give to us when you choose to participate in various activities related to the App, such as registration, profile creation, and contact.

1.2 Fitness and Health Data

The App is designed to help you track your fitness progress. We collect data related to your workouts, weight, height, age, and fitness goals to provide personalized tracking and recommendations.

1.3 Camera Access

Our App requires access to your device's camera for the following purposes:

  • Profile Customization: To allow you to take and upload a profile picture.
  • Content Sharing: To allow you to capture photos of your fitness progress or meals to share within the App community.

Note: We do not collect, store, or process any visual data from your camera without your explicit interaction. We do not use the camera for background tracking or facial recognition.

1.4 Derivative Data

Our servers automatically collect information when you access the App, such as your native actions that are integral to the App, including liking, re-blogging, or replying to a post, as well as other interactions with the App and other users via server log files.

1.5 Financial Data

We use third-party payment processors (such as Razorpay) to handle all payment transactions. We do not store or collect your financial card information. All such data is provided directly to our third-party processor whose use of your personal information is governed by their Privacy Policy.

2. Use of Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the App to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Generate a personal profile for you.
  • Compile anonymous statistical data for use internally or with third parties.
  • Email you regarding your account or order.
  • Enable user-to-user communications.
  • Increase the efficiency and operation of the App.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the App.
